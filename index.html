OUR COMMUNITY

Classes

Rent one of our beautiful classrooms for your next meeting, workshop, or training. Students and facilitators find our classrooms perfect for hackathons and studying. Naturally, the rooms are equipped with all your basic needs such as whiteboards and projectors.

Cafe & Coworking Space

We provide a hip communal work space that gives both the feel of co-working space AND a cafe. The space comes equipped with fast, reliable Wi-Fi, guaranteed access to a desk with an outlet and a community of like-minded professionals.

Partnerships

CoderSchool is partnered with companies such as TinyPulse, LINE, Lazada, and more. Along with inspiring and training the next wave of engineers we desire to cultivate a tech ecosystem for everyone, everywhere. Join us as we strive to become a beacon for the tech industry

Event Space

Situated at the entrance to Coderschool, Modern Hustle is an ideal venue for your next event! Host up to 100 people for hackathons, birthday parties, music workshops, job fairs, and more. Available for both daytime and evening, the event space is fully equipped.
